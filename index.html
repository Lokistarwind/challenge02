About Me

I started out being a go getter coming out of college. I looked for opportunity in expanding my skills and getting on the field experience. As time passed, lots of turmoil in the world and in my personal life, I couldn't find work. In order to compensate, I had to think outside the box to expand. After trying my hand in different vocations such as video editing, running a business, and customer service, I want to get out there and look for technical work. It is still my passion and want an opportunity to be involved. Even if temporary or if its just a chance to hear me out, I still will find it as a learning experience as I attempt to dive into a field that want to work hard on. If I am able to adapt into many things, then being able to work in a new vocation should be a rewarding experience. Thank you for time and have a nice day.
